Ryan Burgess is currently a UI Engineering Manager at Netflix, who's passionate about all things Front End.

Throughout his years of experience he has obtained a great deal of knowledge working for internal and external clients delivering web and mobile applications. He has a extensive experience working with HTML5, CSS3, SASS, LESS, Responsive Design, JavaScript, NodeJS, ReactJS, AngularJS, BackboneJS, JQuery, Mobile, PHP, MySQL, ActionScript 3 and the Adobe Creative Suite.

In his spare time, Ryan is a regular on the Front End Happy Hour podcast.
