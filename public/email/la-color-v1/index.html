LA IN COLOR

VOLUME 1

A photography magazine
by Ryan Burgess

Hi everyone,

First off, thank you again to everyone who purchased SF in Color. It honestly meant a lot to see people connect with the magazine.

Since releasing SF in Color, my family and I moved from San Francisco to Los Angeles, and over the last several months, I’ve spent a lot of time exploring the city, taking photos.

I eventually built up a collection of enough photos that has now become LA in Color Volume 1. This brand new photography magazine is 41-pages documenting my exploration of Los Angeles through color, architecture, and movement as I continue developing my visual style in a completely new environment.

One thing I’ve really come to appreciate through making these magazines is how much they force me to slow down and reflect on my photography. It’s been a great experience turning these periods of exploration into something physical and thoughtfully sequenced.

LA in Color Volume 1 is available now.
